About us:

New Leaf Hospital, Established in November 2022, is a health care institution providing patient treatment with specialized health science and auxiliary healthcare staff and medical equipment.
Departments offered include, but are not limited to our 24/7 Emergency room, working to treat urgent health problems ranging from fire and accident victims to a sudden illness. Our operating rooms, which are top of the line with the best surgeons in the province and bloodbank for those in need. Our staff are always striving to be the best while also offering experimental treatment and medicine.

Our Emergency room is open 24 hours, 7 days a week to ensure anyone dealing with life threatening illness or injury are able to quickly and efficiently get the help they need.

Aside from our Bloodbank and Emergency room, all other departments at the New Leaf Hospital require an appointment to help us schedule around our Call-in doctors.
